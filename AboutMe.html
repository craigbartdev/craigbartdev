About Me

I live in Seattle, Washington and have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Boston University. I am a self-motivated learner and enjoy using online resources like Udemy to increase my knowledge of web development and other topics. Currently, I am learning about Azure and e-commerce development. I am comfortable on both Windows, Mac, and Linux systems, and enjoy configuring and optimizing my Linux desktop. My other hobbies are music production, biking (road and mountain), skiing, and paddleboarding. I particularly enjoy working with audio in Reaper DAW, and have spent much of my time over the past few years learning how to design, produce, and release music and other forms of audio with it.
